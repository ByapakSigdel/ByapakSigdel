## 🧑‍🚀 About me

- 🎓 Electronics engineering student who codes across **embedded · systems · web**
- 🦀 In love with the low level — a hobby OS in Rust (**NexOS**), CLIs in Go & Rust, firmware in C/C++
- 🎧 Latest build: **[scratch](https://github.com/ByapakSigdel/scratch)** — a DJ-turntable scratch engine in raw C with a Python testbench

- 🌐 More of my work lives at **[mahansigdel.com.np](https://mahansigdel.com.np/)**
